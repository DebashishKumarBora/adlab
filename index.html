£The core formulas

        
Every number is computed live in your browser. Nothing is faked.

        
Clicks = Spend ÷ CPC
Conversions = Clicks × Conversion rate
Revenue = Conversions × AOV
ROAS = Revenue ÷ Spend
Net profit = (Conversions × AOV × Margin) − Spend
Break-even ROAS = 1 ÷ Margin

        
Break-even ROAS is the line everything hangs on: at a 50% margin you need 2x ROAS just to wash your face. Below it, more spend means more loss.
